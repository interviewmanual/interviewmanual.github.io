Rules for a New Era

Understand the nuances of remote interviews and
gain a greater understanding of recruiters techniques.

  • Eye Contact: The Golden Triangle

    Keep your focus near your camera to maintain eye contact and avoid looking distracted.

  • Get the Composition Right

    Place your laptop on books to increase the height to get a natural conversational angle, rather than up your nose.

  • Understand Question Types

    Competency-based, strength-based, behavioural or personal questions, the manual covers them.

  • Troubleshooting & Testing

    Guides on troubleshooting common issues and tutorials for creating test meetings to check audio & video.
